Driving Directions

The Redmond Community Center at Marymoor Village

6505 176th Ave NE,
Redmond, WA 98052


Directions from I-405

  1. Take exit 14 to merge onto 520 E - Toward Redmond (go 5.8 mi)
  2. Take the 202 /Redmond Way exit (go 0.5 mi), you will need to be in one of the right 2 lanes
  3. Turn right onto 202/Redmond Way (go 7 miles)
  4. Take a slight right onto Lake Sammamish Pkwy NE -- it is right after Whole Foods store on your right. (go 0.1 mile)
  5. Turn right onto NE 65th -- this is right after the veterinary hospital that is on your right side (go 0.3 miles)
  6. Drive to the Redmond Community Center (building is on the corner of NE 65th and 176th NE)
  7. Parking is available on both sides of the building and on the street.

I'm at the community center...

Head up the main stairs or take the elevator to the second floor.
Look for the registration table in the hallway.

Community Room, Spokane Valley Mall

14700 E Indiana Ave,

I'm at the mall...

Enter on the west side of the mall, near Sears, and go to the second floor.
The community room is in the far southwest corner of the mall, near the children's play area.
